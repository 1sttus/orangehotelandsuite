During calibration, technicians compare the performance output of a measuring tool or instrument with a measurement standard. Calibration services confirm that the item will produce results which meet or exceed that standard's criteria.

Our professional calibration staff works with state-of-the-art calibration equipment in an environment- controlled laboratory so you can count on precise results and quick turnaround.

Kuest Int'l Company offers three types of instrument calibration services to help companies avoid test and measurement equipment failures, while meeting auditable documentation requirements
We build and maintained a successful calibration partnerships OEM and leading solution partners across the globe for capacity improvement as well as for dynamic and intelligent and quality instrument calibration processes and measurement expertise. Contact us on phone/email for your instrument calibration needs.

  • LSOL7O25 ACCREDITED CALIBRATION:

If you're concerned about quality standards and want to receive the most comprehensive cal data, then use we adopt ISO 17025 Accredited Calibration service. This service is available on most the clients' newer products. These calibrations include a calibration certificate and measurement report, indicating “before” (as found) and “after” (as left) data with measurement uncertainties. All measurements are traceable to the appropriate national and international standards organizations. To learn more, you can contact us for our guidelines on how we perform our accredited calibrations.

  • CALIBRATION WITH DATA:

If you're not concerned about the extra value offered by an accredited Calibration with Data, available on most products, includes a calibration certificate and measurement report indicating “before” (as found) and “after” (as left) data. All measurements are traceable to the appropriate national and international standards organizations. Most of these calibrations are performed in compliance with calibration standard ANSI/NCSL Z540-1. (Please note that before data is not typically available on products in need of repair.)
