DREDGING, MARINE & OIL RIG SERVICES

KUEST INTERNATIONAL COMPANY, is an established provider of premium marine logistic support in Engineering / Construction, Equipment Rental / Leasing, Procurement / Supply and General Labor service provider to the ever expanding clientele comprising Government, Oil and Gas Companies and other private corporate organizations (including individuals/ corporate firms).
Since inception, we have built a reputation by passionately and professionally executing our jobs/ contracts in order to deliver the best value to our clients. In so doing, we have positioned our company as a premier marine organization in virtually all our areas of operations.
KUEST INTERNATIONAL COMPANY, also enjoys a reputation for bringing vision, innovation, well design programs and integrity to all its operations in the dredging and marine logistic sectors of the Oil and Gas Industry. A unique combination of local and foreign professionals to deliver excellent and cost-effective solutions to our client problems. We have a policy of involving the best available locals, in all trades, with rigorous supervision which provides us safe working environment without compromising on quality in the field of dredging, reclamation, and general marine logistic works.
Among a whole wide range of marine services carry out by Kuest International Company, we also carry out the following services;

  • General Dredging services
  • Sea reclamation services
  • Sea-sure protection services
  • General Marine services
  • Marine Logistics support services
  • Deep offshore marine Logistic Services
  • hiring of Crew boat, Tug boat, Security boat, House boat and Barges
  • Water and food supply to offshore locations
  • Oil Rig movement services
  • Oil Rig / work site preparation (Bond work) services
  • Facilitate the hiring, Leasing or procurement of Oil Rigs
  • Vessel & Tanker hiring services
