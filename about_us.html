Welcome to our Company

KUEST INTERNATIONAL COMPANY is a focused, competent, incorporated in 1993 with head office in Warri, Delta State delivering a wide range of Safety, Civil and Mechanical Engineering, Oil and Gas field services/management to the Nigerian Oil and Construction industry. A focused, competent and well- motivated management and staff have guided and built this company into a proven and quality focused company.

KUEST INTERNATIONAL COMPANY has clearly defined and ambitious targets in technical, managerial and H.S.E standards which have been tested and proven by our competent management team and dedicated staff.

We hope you will continue to demand our services as we are very technically proficient and happy to provide them. It our target to offer superior value and excellence to our clients in every aspect of work /contract in terms of completion time, cost and overall quality.

MISSION

KUEST INTERNATIONAL COMPANY is to become the leading innovative solution provider offering statutory third party inspection, Testing & Certification, Calibration and Training, engineering, project management and technical support services to the upstream and downstream sector of the Nigerian Oil and Gas industry, this will be achieved through a dedicated and committed team of highly skilled professional and the use of strategic alliance with highly reputable international OEMs (Partners) who have expertise and experience in same line of business to boast quality and timely services delivery.

Also to contribute to the development of human capacity industry with a view to enhancing the development of skill and expertise through accredited internationally recognized training courses, within Oil & Gas/ Non-Oil & Gas sectors of Nigerian economy and beyond, while ensuring customer satisfaction and continual improvement on our processes & service

VISION

KUEST INTERNATIONAL COMPANY aims to become one of the best and most competent Engineering, Oilfield and gas services companies in Nigeria with core competencies in highly complex contracts involving a multiplicity of skills and capabilities. This means that as your requirements – technological, social, environmental along with safety considerations evolve, we can evolve with you, anticipating your needs, sharing the risk, establishing performance targets, and sticking to them. This enables us to deliver future – proof solutions at a competitive price with the same high level of competence no matter where.
