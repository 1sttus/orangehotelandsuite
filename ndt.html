NDT / API INSPECTION

  • NDT INSPECTION SOLUTION

KUEST INT'L COMPANY offers a wide inspection solution for clients in spanning (oil & Gas and General) sectors simplifying Inspection challenges by engaging certified professionals with Innovative technologies to bring about qualitative and timely solutions:

KUEST INT'L COMPANY   is licensed and insured to provide non-destructive testing and engineering service to the energy industry throughout the Four Corners of Nigeria and beyond covering wider applications. Our registered professional engineers, certified inspectors and technicians evaluate materials, piping, metallic and nonmetallic structure members, machineries, facilities, facilities, pressure vessels and tanks using acceptable national and international standards for optimum goal of ensuring the availability and integrity of the systems; and most cases for proper design and safety, anchorage and repairs or alternations. Our highly skilled professionals are supported by the industry's best training, equipment, and technology. Safety is our priority - our record proves it.

We cover all the principles of NDT Technologies for Mechanical and Metallurgical testing as follows:
Ultrasonic Immersion Inspection
Ultrasonic Contact Inspection
Radiographic Inspection
Eddy Current Inspection
Mechanical / Metallurgical services
Dye Penetrant Inspection (Red & Fluorescent)

Grain Size, Corrosion, Ferrite, Macro, Micro, Failure Investigation etc.
