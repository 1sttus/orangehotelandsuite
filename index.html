WHAT WE DO!

Kuest Int'l Company primarily provides marine / logistic, engineering, dredging, procurement, construction, installation and consultancy services on a turnkey basis for energy development projects. Our scope of operations covers various types of engineering services including:

  • Oil Storage Tanks Construction
  • Welding & Fabrication
  • Scalfolding services
  • Piling
  • Pipeline Welding and Repair
  • Environmental protection services
  • Safety Solution
  • Calibration and metrological services
  • NDT/API Inspections
  • Corrosion and control Services
  • Lagging and Insulation
  • Dredging and Marine Services
  • Civil/Mechanical Engineering
  • Construction and Maintenance Services
  • Labour Supply
  • General Contracts/Consultans
