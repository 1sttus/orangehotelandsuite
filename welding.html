PIPELLINE WELDING AND MAINTAINANCE

Kuest International Company is dedicated to achieving professional and timely outcomes for clients' projects on pipework fabrication and installation, testing and drying, rehabilitation, and composite reinforcement. We have many years of experience in the pipeline field and have carried out projects for clients all around the world.
The increased demands on pipeline and riser specifications call for compatible advances in welding and inspection technology.
These increase demands has driven Kuest International Company to offer one in class, and also work to take the lead in pipeline welding and inspection environment, in all pipeline welding specifications, as may be differ from applications and systems.

We have an extensive ongoing welding Research and Development program to enhance fatigue life and improve the quality of performance across a range of pipeline materials including conventional carbon steel, corrosion-resistant alloys and high-strength steel applications.

WELDING INSPECTIONh

The need to inspect and test pipelines and pipework is ever increasing Kuest International Company have recognized that a fully equipped mobile test unit can solve many of the problems associated with hydrostatic and pneumatic testing on site. All the testing equipment used is calibrated to recognized standards and the fully trained personnel are experienced in all aspects of testing pipelines and pipework. The test unit is capable of monitoring tests up to 4000 psi. A comprehensive test report can be provided to validate the test. Drying of pipelines is also an increasing aspect of pipeline validation, Kuest International Company offer a vacuum drying service whereby the pipeline/pipe work can be dried to the required DP.
